<p><i>mulint(var, int)</i></p>

<p>
Parameters:<br>
1: Integer variable<br>
2: Integer
</p>

<p>
Description:<br>
Multiplies parameter 1 by parameter 2, and stores it in parameter 1.
</p>

<p>
Code (see description):<br>
<pre>
mov al, [ptr]
mov ah, <val>

mov [ptr],ax
